## Deflationary Mechanisms

### Transaction Tax (1%)
Every on-chain transaction incurs a 1% tax:
- **40%** → Token burn (permanent supply reduction)
- **30%** → AGI development fund
- **20%** → Staker rewards
- **10%** → Ecosystem treasury

### AGI Buyback Program
- AGI-generated revenue (trading fees, service fees) used to buy $PNX from market
- Purchased tokens are burned quarterly
- Target: 0.5% supply burn per year

### Mining Halving
- Mining rewards halve every 2 years
- Schedule:
  - Year 0-2: 100% emission rate
  - Year 2-4: 50% emission rate
  - Year 4-6: 25% emission rate
  - Year 6+: 12.5% (approaching asymptote)
